About Us

Team Kratos Racing is a Formula Student team from Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering, featuring 40 dedicated students from various departments including Mechanical, EnTC, Computer, and Civil. We design, build, and test Formula 3-style vehicles, promoting skill development and aiming for excellence in motorsports. Since 2015, we've created three combustion and one electric Formula vehicle, earning multiple national championships.

Our electric vehicle, Thunderblade, won Formula Bharat 2019 with a record score, and in 2020, we developed India's first full CFRP monocoque chassis. Even during the pandemic, we continued our winning streak. Driven by the motto "TOGETHER WE RISE," we strive to be a symbol of teamwork, innovation, and success in Formula Student competitions.
